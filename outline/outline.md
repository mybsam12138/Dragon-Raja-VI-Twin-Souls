# Dragon Raja VI — Twin Souls

## Theme

Who are we?

Not reincarnation.

Not destiny.

Not gods.

Not monsters.

Only souls searching for their missing half.

---

# Prologue

## The Girl Beneath the Cherry Blossoms

Several years after Dragon Raja V.

A young girl lives quietly in northern Japan.

She occasionally dreams of:

* Falling cherry blossoms
* Tokyo Station
* A train ticket
* A boy she cannot remember

She always wakes before seeing his face.

Yet every dream ends the same way.

A departing train.

A crowded platform.

And a question that breaks her heart for reasons she cannot understand.

"Will you come back?"

She dismisses it as a dream.
